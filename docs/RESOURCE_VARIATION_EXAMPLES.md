# Resource Variation Examples

## Specialized Asteroid Examples

### Example 1: Normal C-Type Asteroid (Inner System)
**Name:** Asteroid 2024-Alpha  
**Location:** 2.8 AU (main belt)  
**Mass:** 5×10¹⁷ kg  
**Type:** Standard C-type

**Resources:**
- Iron: 18% (90,000 Mt) - Moderate
- Silicates: 32% (160,000 Mt) - Common
- Aluminum: 7% (35,000 Mt) - Moderate
- Gold: 0.0000003% (1.5 Mt) - Trace
- Platinum: 0.00000002% (0.1 Mt) - Ultra rare
- Water: Absent (skipped)
- Copper: Absent (skipped)

**Notes:** Typical rocky asteroid. Some precious metals but very scarce. Not a priority mining target.

## Special Body Examples

### Example 5: Europa (Jupiter's Moon)
**Name:** Europa  
**Location:** 5.2 AU (Jupiter orbit)  
**Mass:** 4.8×10²² kg  
**Type:** Ice moon with subsurface ocean  
**Profile:** SPECIAL - Europa profile applied

**Resources:**
- **Water: 85% (4.08×10¹³ Mt)** - MASSIVE ocean!
- Oxygen: 5% (2.4×10¹² Mt) - In ice
- Silicates: 8% (3.84×10¹² Mt) - Rocky core
- Iron: 2% (9.6×10¹¹ Mt) - Small core
- Everything else: Absent

**Accessibility Notes:**
- Water accessibility: 40% (need to drill through ice shell)
- Once accessed, effectively unlimited water supply
- Strategic location for outer system colonization

**Notes:** Single most important water source in outer solar system. One moon contains more accessible water than hundreds of asteroids combined.

## Strategic Implications

### Early Game (Inner System)
- **Target:** Mars, Moon, carbonaceous asteroids
- **Goal:** Water, construction materials, He-3
- **Strategy:** Establish self-sufficient colonies

### Mid Game (Belt Mining)
- **Target:** Main belt asteroids, hunt for specializations
- **Goal:** Find platinum-rich or gold-rich asteroids
- **Strategy:** Prospect many asteroids, mine the best

### Late Game (Outer System)
- **Target:** Europa, Enceladus, Titan
- **Goal:** Massive water reserves, exotic volatiles
- **Strategy:** Establish fuel depots for deep space missions

### End Game (Economy)
- **Control:** Specialized asteroids = economic power
- **Trade:** Platinum and gold drive interplanetary commerce
- **Expansion:** Water sources enable colonization of next star system
